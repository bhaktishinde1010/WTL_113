With our client's projects and through our innovations, we create solutions that are used worldwide - over land and sea. Our management systems support the operation of more than 1,800 vessels, from tugs to tankers and rigs. We ensure that critical development and management activities can continue in the most challenging of environments.

Management Systems

Trainingportal

Our market-leading, cloud-based system, Trainingportal enables efficient delivery, management, and reporting of workforce training and competence remotely and from one centralised solution. Trainingportal combines both an LMS and a CMS and can be integrated with existing HR and ERP systems.

Trainingportal

OCS HR

A complete crew management solution enabling you to deploy the right people in the right place at the right time and maximising operational efficiency. The system combines employee data, crew rotation planning, and competence needs while integrating payroll, training, and travel booking to allow for seamless operations.

Marketplace
