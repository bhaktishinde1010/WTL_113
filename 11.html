About SSK Wardrobe

Where tradition meets contemporary fashion

Our Story

Founded in 2015, SSK Wardrobe began as a small boutique in Kopargaon with a vision to bring elegant, affordable fashion to women across Maharashtra. Today, we've grown into a beloved brand known for our quality craftsmanship and unique designs.

Our name 'SSK' represents the three sisters who founded this brand - Sunita, Sarika, and Kavita. Their passion for fashion and commitment to quality continues to inspire everything we do.

We specialize in blending traditional Indian textiles with contemporary silhouettes, creating pieces that honor our heritage while embracing modern style sensibilities.

Quality Craftsmanship

Each garment is carefully crafted with attention to detail and quality materials.

Ethical Production

We work with artisans and ensure fair wages and safe working conditions.

Nationwide Delivery

Free shipping across India with easy returns and exchanges.
